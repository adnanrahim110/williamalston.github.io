About the Author

Dr. William G. Alston: Pioneering the Path to Optimal Human Potential William G. Alston, PhD, is a renowned expert in the fields of human performance, stress management, and holistic wellbeing. With over 40 years of experience as a researcher, author, and speaker, Dr. Alston has dedicated his career to unlocking the secrets of the natural anabolic state and empowering individuals to reach their full potential. His groundbreaking work has helped countless people from all walks of life achieve remarkable physical, mental, and spiritual transformation.
